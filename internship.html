Research Based Intenships at the Indian Institute of Science

Transliteration of Kannada Text in a Camera Captured Scene Image on an Android Platform

I worked on deriving a pre-processing algorithm for raw, textual scene images to suit the requirements for OCRs like Tesseract. The Tesseract OCR was trained separately with combinations of various Kannada fonts for optimal recognition of the Kannada Script. The final step comprised building an Android application that pre-processed the image and transliterated the textual content with the help of Tesseract tools for Android. The best training data combination was chosen for the same. I was also introduced toneural network architectures for recognition problem statements.I interned under Professor K.R. Ramakrishnan and was mentored by Pavan Kulkarni.

Steerable Fast Bilateral Edge Detectors

Although several techniques have been used to obtain computationally fast bilateral filters,I implemented an algorithm having a reasonable trade-off between image accuracy and computational time. We proposed a noise- robust and computationally efficient algorithm for steerable fast bilateral edge detection using the concepts of steerability, edge detection via bilateral filters and fast bilateral filtering using Fourier kernels. I designed two new algorithms based on fast bilateral edge detectors and steerable fast bilateral edge detectors.
